Find your beauty type!

The 4 seasons colour analysis theory is based on four colour types: Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter. Which seasonal type you are depends on two basic variables:

  • the undertone of your skin, hair and eyes (warm/golden vs cool/ashy)
  • how light vs deep your overall colouring and specifically your hair is

The seasons represent the four possible variations of these two variables: If your natural hair colour is lighter than medium brown, you would be typed as either a Spring or a Summer; if itâ€™s darker you are a Winter or an Autumn. If your skin tone and hair have a warm undertone, or you are a natural red-head, you would be classed as either a Spring or an Autumn; if your skin has a blue-ish, cool undertone and your hair is more ashy and has no golden or red highlights, you are either a Summer or a Winter. When you know your beauty type, you can then choose colours of makeup that is most flattering for you!
